About me

I’m a multi-disciplinary designer with over 20 years of experience, including more than a decade architecting design systems, most recently at LinkedIn, supporting hundreds of designers and thousands of engineers, across multiple consumer and enterprise products.

I build robust, scalable, and accessible design systems that bridge brand identity and product experience. I’m passionate about inclusive design and fostering collaborative partnerships between design and engineering partners, leading to the efficient delivery of high-quality, cohesive, and user-centric experiences.

My contributions include:

  • Comprehensive design token libraries that incorporate theme and mode-switching capabilities to ensure cross-platform consistency and WCAG-compliant component libraries
  • Creation and evolution of systematic color palettes and patterns that can be utilized by cross-functional design teams and partners (product, brand, marketing, external vendors)
  • Scalable typographic systems that are intuitive and can adapt to any device, screen size, and locale (including non-Latin script considerations)
  • Extensive iconography sets, drawing influence from brand identity and relevant product features
  • Creative direction and education for my immediate team and org-wide design partners

Hit me up if you’re looking for an expert to bring your design system to the next level.
